# Marketplace SEO Differences

> Type: Reference — detailed breakdown of marketplace search algorithms

## Shopify (Google Organic)

Shopify stores rank in Google search. The algorithm is Google's standard:
- Backlinks and domain authority are the #1 factor
- Content quality and depth matter significantly
- Core Web Vitals are a ranking signal
- Mobile-first indexing (Google crawls mobile version)
- Collection pages rank better than product pages for generic terms
- Blog content builds topical authority over time
- Schema markup enables rich results (stars, price, availability)

## Etsy Search Algorithm

Etsy uses a proprietary search algorithm with these known factors:
- **Query matching**: Title, tags, categories, attributes (exact + broad match)
- **Listing quality score**: Based on conversion rate (views -> favorites -> purchases)
- **Recency**: Recently listed/renewed items get a temporary boost
- **Customer and market experience**: Shop reviews, complete policies, response time
- **Shipping price**: Free or low shipping is rewarded
- **Relevancy**: Category Relevance Score — how well your listing matches the search category
- **Personalization**: Etsy shows different results to different shoppers based on behavior

Etsy-specific tips:
- Renew listings strategically for the recency boost
- Use all 13 tags with multi-word phrases
- Complete all listing attributes
- Respond to messages within 24 hours
- Maintain shop review rating above 4.5

## Amazon A9/COSMO Algorithm

Amazon's search algorithm prioritizes purchase likelihood:
- **Sales velocity**: Recent sales volume is the strongest signal
- **Conversion rate**: Click-to-purchase ratio
- **Price competitiveness**: Compared to similar products
- **Reviews**: Count and average rating (4.0+ threshold for visibility)
- **Fulfillment method**: FBA (Fulfilled by Amazon) ranks higher than FBM
- **Keyword relevance**: Title, bullet points, backend keywords
- **Inventory**: In-stock items rank higher than out-of-stock
- **A+ Content**: Enhanced product pages with rich media

Amazon-specific tips:
- First 80 characters of title are most important (mobile cutoff)
- Use all 5 bullet points with natural keyword inclusion
- Backend keywords: synonyms only, 250-byte limit, no punctuation
- Encourage reviews via Amazon's Request a Review button
- Consider PPC (Sponsored Products) to build initial sales velocity
